Plan Your Visit

Your First Market Day

Everything you need to know before your first visit to Achill Island Market.

🅿️

Parking

Cashel: free car park beside Ted's Bar. Keel: public car park at Keel Beach, two-minute walk. Both have disabled access parking.

⏰

Best Time to Arrive

Gates open at 10am. For the best selection, arrive before 11am. The lunch rush at noon brings the liveliest atmosphere.

🎒

What to Bring

Reusable bags, cash (some vendors are card-only, some cash-only), layers for the weather, and an appetite for local food.

📍

Finding Us

Cashel: Ted's Bar on the R319 — look for the market flags. Keel: at the heart of Keel village, beside the beach car park.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Friendly

Buggy-accessible paths, kids love the craft stalls, and there's always treats and hot chocolate. A proper family morning out.

🐕

Pet Policy

Well-behaved dogs are welcome on a lead. Water bowls available. Please clean up after your pet — bags provided at the entrance.
