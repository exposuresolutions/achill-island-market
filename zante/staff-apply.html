How the quest works

You always know what’s next. Interviews are a two-way briefing: we say what we expect, you say what you need — then you show us you can do the job.

  1. 1–2

    Stages 1 & 2 — Online checkpoint

    Stage 1: This form — who you are, venues, Greece XP.
    Stage 2: Your video + written pitch (why you, why us, what you add, why Zante). You’re here now — finish the form to save your checkpoint.

  2. 3

    Stage 3 — Co-op interview

    Virtual call: expectations on both sides, culture fit, availability, and straight answers. No tricks — just clarity before anyone flies.

  3. 4

    Stage 4 — Boss stage (in Zante)

    Come to the island and meet the team for real. On-site trial / floor moment: show your skills — welcome, energy, service instinct, how you read a busy room. That’s the “final boss” — real customers, real venue, real you.
