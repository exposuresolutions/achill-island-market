How Achill Island Market Works

Whether you're a vendor looking to sell or a customer looking to shop — here's everything you need to know.

How to Get a Stall

From application to market day — here's the full process.

1

Fill in the Application Form

Head to our vendor registration page and tell us about yourself and what you sell. Pick your stall type, preferred market location (Cashel and/or Keel) and how often you can attend. Takes about 2 minutes.

2

We Review & Get in Touch

We'll review your application within 48 hours and give you a call or text to confirm your spot, discuss stall location and answer any questions.

3

Your Vendor Page Goes Live

We create a dedicated page for your stall on achillstore.store with your description, products, location and contact info. This gets you found on Google and gives customers a way to discover you before market day.

4

We Promote You

Your stall gets featured on our Facebook, Instagram and website. We announce the weekly vendor lineup every Tuesday so customers know who's coming.

5

Show Up & Sell!

Cashel: Every Wednesday, 10am–4pm at Ted's Bar. Keel: Every Saturday. Bring your products, we provide the footfall and promotion. Large carpark, full bar and facilities on-site.

6

Online Sales (Every Saturday)

We're building online ordering so customers can pre-order your products and collect at the market or get them posted. Opt in during registration and we'll set you up when it launches.

💰

What Does It Cost?

Stall fees vary by location and size. We'll discuss pricing when we confirm your spot. It's affordable and designed to support local producers.

🕙

When & Where?

Cashel: Every Wednesday, 10am–4pm at Ted's Bar, Cashel, Achill Island.
Keel: Every Saturday — register your interest now.

🧑‍🍳

Who Can Apply?

Anyone who grows, bakes, makes or creates something. Food producers, crafters, artists, plant sellers, jewellery makers — all welcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the market?
Our Cashel market is held at Ted's Bar, Cashel, Achill Island, Co. Mayo (F28 AX57). Large free carpark on-site. Our Keel location is Every Saturday — stay tuned for updates.
When is market day?
Cashel market runs every Wednesday from 10am to 4pm, all year round. Keel market dates will be announced soon.
How much does a stall cost?
Stall fees vary depending on location and size. We keep it affordable to support local producers. Apply and we'll discuss pricing when we confirm your spot.
Can I sell at both Cashel and Keel?
Absolutely! When you apply, you can select both locations. Many vendors sell at both markets.
I'm not from Achill — can I still get a stall?
Yes! We welcome producers and makers from across Mayo and Ireland. Many of our vendors come from the mainland. As long as you grow it, bake it, make it or create it — there's a spot for you.
How does online ordering work?
We're building online ordering so customers can browse vendor products, place an order and either collect at the market on Wednesday or have items posted. It's Every Saturday — subscribe to our newsletter for updates.
Do I need insurance to sell at the market?
We recommend public liability insurance for all vendors. If you're selling food, you'll need to comply with food safety regulations. We can discuss requirements when we confirm your application.
Can I pay by card at the market?
Most vendors accept card payments, but it's worth bringing some cash too. Ted's Bar accepts all major cards.
