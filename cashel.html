Achill Island Market — Cashel

Every Wednesday at Ted's Bar, Cashel, Achill Island. We'd love to have you. Whether you grow it, bake it, make it or create it — there's a spot for you here. We also have a Keel location — apply for either!

  • 📍 Cashel location — Ted's Bar carpark, Achill Island
  • 🌿 Food producers, crafters, artists, bakers, plant sellers all welcome
  • 📣 We promote every vendor on this website & social media
  • 🎬 Video feature on our market website for every stall
  • ☕ Full bar, food & facilities on-site at Ted's
  • 🚗 Large carpark — easy for customers to park
